Challenge

Dan John, founded in Rome, 1855. The celebrated Italian fashion brand, renowned for their distinctive style and contemporary collections, desperately needed a fresh eCommerce site fit for the 21st century. A site that represented the brand's long heritage in the industry yet with a crisp contemporary edge....much like a fine Italian suit.

What We Did?

Working with the Dan John team, we needed to fuse the latest headless technology with their 170 years of tradition, providing their online clientele with the same impeccable in-store service experience.

Enhanced Navigation

We created a user-centric navigation system that allows customers to effortlessly explore Dan John's extensive range of products. Our design ensures that shoppers can easily find what they're looking for, whether it's a classic Italian suit or a modern accessory. We organised products into clearly defined categories, making it simple for visitors to browse through the collection.

Headless Development

The Dan John team wanted a website that not only looked stunning but also performed flawlessly. With headless development, we were able to achieve just that. We separated the design and user interface from the backend, enabling faster load times, smoother navigation, and enhanced functionality. Headless architecture ensures that the website is ready for omnichannel retailing. Customers can shop from any device, whether it's a desktop, smartphone, or tablet.

Online Look Builder

Imagine a virtual dressing room where you can mix and match different pieces of clothing, experiment with styles, and curate your perfect outfit without physically trying anything on. Dan John wanted to bridge the gap between the physical store experience and online shopping, and the Online Look Builder was styles, and Dan John benefits from extended website visits.
